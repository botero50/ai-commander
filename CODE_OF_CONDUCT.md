# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in the AI Commander project and community will be treated with respect.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Demonstrating professionalism in all interactions

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Offensive comments related to gender, sexual orientation, disability, mental illness, neuroype, physical appearance, body size, race, or religion
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Deliberate intimidation or threats
- Sustained disruption of discussion or events
- Advocating for, or encouraging, any of the above behaviors

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces online and offline:

- GitHub repositories and issues
- Pull request discussions
- Community forums and discussions
- Social media interactions related to the project
- In-person events and conferences
- Any other space where the community gathers

This Code of Conduct also applies to representations of the project in the wider community.

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior.

### Reporting Violations

If you witness or are subject to behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to conduct@anthropic.com with:

- Description of the incident
- Who was involved
- When and where it occurred
- Context and how you were affected
- Any evidence or witnesses

**All reports are confidential.** Reporters will not be identified without their consent.

### Investigation Process

1. **Receipt:** We will acknowledge your report within 48 hours
2. **Investigation:** We will investigate the incident thoroughly
3. **Response:** We will respond to the reporter with findings (within reason for confidentiality)
4. **Action:** Appropriate action will be taken to address the violation
5. **Follow-up:** We will follow up with parties involved as appropriate

### Consequences

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

- Warning from community leaders
- Temporary suspension from participation
- Permanent removal from the community
- Referral to appropriate authorities (for illegal behavior)

The severity of consequences will be proportional to the nature and severity of the violation.

## Appeal Process

If you believe an enforcement action was unjust, you may appeal to conduct@anthropic.com within 30 days of the decision. The appeal will be reviewed by a different community leader or team member.
