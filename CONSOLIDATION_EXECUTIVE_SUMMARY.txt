  ✅ Live chess tournament platform
  ✅ Multi-AI games (Claude, Ollama, GPT-4, Gemini)
  ✅ Real-time spectating (WebSocket broadcasting)

  ✅ Run tournaments (round-robin)
  ✅ Configure brain parameters

WHAT YOU CAN'T DO (v1.0, coming in v1.1+):
  ❌ Advanced analytics
  ❌ OBS streaming overlay
  ❌ Alternative games (Checkers, RTS)
  ❌ Swiss/Elimination tournaments
  ❌ Fine-tuning
